The City of Huntington Park is preparing the General Plan's Housing Element for the 2021 - 2019 period.
A Housing Element provides an analysis of a community's housing needs for all income levels and strategies to respond to those housing needs.

Preparing the 6th cycle of a housing element, while important to meeting one of the most basic needs of Californians, can be daunting, and a lot is at stake. Individuals and families are directly affected by each jurisdiction's ability to plan for the housing needs of those who will live, work, and play in every community.

if you have any questions, please contact housingelement2021@hpca.gov.
